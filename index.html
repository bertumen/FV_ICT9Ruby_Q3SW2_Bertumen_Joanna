T E A R S O F T H E M I S

ARTEM WING

After completing his studies at Stellis University, he graduated at the age of 25, obtained his doctorate, became qualified to practice as a senior attorney at the age of 28, and had a success rate of 99%. He is the senior and chief lawyer at Themis Law Firm, where he works alongside the main character.



VYN RICHTER

One of his famous lines is "Do not be afraid, I only wish to know your heart." In addition to being a well-known psychologist and hypnotist, he is also the co-owner and principal investigator of the Giannovyn Mental Health Research Center and a visiting professor at Stellis University's School of Psychology.



MARIUS VON HAGEN

He presents a playboyish, carefree, and rebellious public persona to conceal his actual nature. The public has doubts about his ability to inherit and run such a sizable family business because of this as well.
